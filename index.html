About Me

I am an aspiring UX/UI designer with a strong foundation in fine arts, blending creativity, problem-solving, and user-centered thinking into every project I take on. I enjoy empathizing with people and listening to their needs, doing what I can to make all experiences seamless, inclusive, and meaningful.

I am pursuing a B.S. in User Experience & Interaction Design at Drexel University, graduating in May 2029. In my education, I am developing the necessary design, research, and technical skills to bring positive user-focused digital experiences to life.

My Background

My interest in the arts began at a very young age. As a toddler, my mom would find me on the living room floor with my pencils, completely absorbed in creating. My fascination with art-making stayed with me throughout my childhood, and by seventh grade I began preparing for New York City's competitive arts high schools auditions.

Portfolio pieces made in 7th grade in preparation for specialized arts high school auditions.

The process of preparing lasted a year and a half and was was intense and high-pressure time, sometimes feeling like the college application process at the ripe age of twelve. It was a period of deep commitment, teaching me early on how much I valued design in my life, and the effort was willing to put in to pursue it.

The Tony Bennett Frank Sinatra School of the Arts in Astoria Queens, NY

In 2020, I was accepted into the Frank Sinatra School of the Arts. My highschool provided rigorous courses and a supportive environment to grow as an artist. I strengthened my abilities in creative problem-solving, visual storytelling, and clear design communication. My classmates and I forged close connections, many of whom have gone on to successful careers in the arts.

Frank Sinatra School of the Arts Senior Art Show

I continue to appreciate traditional art and the skills I have developed over my lifetime, but I am drawn towards this field where I may combine creativity and problem-solving in a more impactful way. UX./UI design is appealing to me because it channels empathetic instincts with design sensibilities into edveloping better experiences that help others. For me, UX/UI is a way to take the passion for creating into fostering beautiful interactive experiences.
